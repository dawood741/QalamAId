How It Works

A simple, transparent process for both students and donors.

For Students
📄

Submit Application

Fill out the application form with your personal info, university details, and upload required documents.

✅

Get Verified

Our team reviews and verifies your application, university enrollment, and financial need.

💵

Receive Funding

Once verified, donors can contribute to your scholarship. Funds are sent directly to your university.

🚀

Pursue Your Dream

Continue your education and share progress updates with your supporters.

For Donors
👀

Browse Students

Browse verified student profiles, read their stories, and find simple info packages.

❤️

Make a Donation

Choose a specific or recurring donation. You can fund a specific student or the general fund.

📦

Funds Delivered

Your donation goes directly to the student’s university tuition, ensuring full transparency.

📈

Track Impact

Receive updates on the student’s progress and see the real impact of your contribution.
